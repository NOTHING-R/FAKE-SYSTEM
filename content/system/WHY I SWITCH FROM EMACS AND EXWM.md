# Why I Switch

Ami j program tar upor prai 1 bocor er moto smy dilam jate ami proti ta line customize kore akta desktop envornment banalam oita k kno carlam!!

## First reason (I want to use the full screen of my system)

Ami asole amr system er full screen ta use korte caitam sob smy e. J kno distraction thakbe na kiccu na. To ami jani na j jinis ta faltu lagbe ki na onno der kace. Kintu amr distraction free jinis besi valo lage j ami ja kortaci sudhu oita....akhn oneke bolbe j vai hudai ato smy nosto...apni to caile e fulscreen kore use korte parten. Ha partam....kintu ote akta smosssa hocce j extra keybind use kora lagto amr....j jinis ta ami caccilam na....r o EMACS ba EXWM er bottom echo area r doom line oita hide korte partecilam na poperly. Oita hoito fix kore feltam kintu tarpreo r o akta reason cilo oitar picone r oita e sob theke boro karon.

## Second reason (Using the text editor as a IDE)

Ami onk try korci emacs a as a ide use korar jonno. Mainly practice er jonno coto code nia experiment korar jonno vscode akta valo solution kkhonoi na. Oitar jonno lightweight kicu akta lage j ta amr kace cilo emacs. Everything at one place. Kintu mojar bepar hocce j ami joto e caici oita k as a ide use korar jonno toto bar e seka khaici. Oita thik thak e cilo amr jonno. Ami sob kicu onk valo vabe korte partam mainly note nite onk subidha hoito. Kintu pore akta smy jeye code lekhar jonno amr jhamela hoite suru kore. Ay jonno emacs bad diye neovim mainly lazyvim a smy dea suru kori. Tarpore aste aste lazyvim ta k akhn fully customize kore `FAKE VIM` baniye felci. Amr emacs use korar sob theke boro karon cilo `ORG` mode r oitar sob kicu. R ami org mode a ovbostho cilam markdwon use kortam na oto ta. Oto ta ki kortam e na .  amr sob kicu amonki README.md tar jaigi README.org use kortam. To bepar da cilo hocce ay. Kintu pore akhn aste aste md te shift korci r org lage na bolle e cole karon org diye mainly emacs diye r kicu kora hoi na.
